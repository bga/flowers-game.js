  • New Game

  • Controlling

    • Keyboard

      left - left

      right - right

      shift or space - shoot

      esc - toggle pause/menu

    • Mouse

      mouse left - left

      mouse right - right

      left click - shoot

      right click - toggle pause/menu

    • Touch

      touch - fire

      touched vertical position - position to move ship

      gesture up/down - pause/menu
